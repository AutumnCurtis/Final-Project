Agatha Christie Books!

Agatha Christie is one of the most influential and widely read authors in literary history, best known for her mystery novels and unforgettable detectives. Born in 1890 in England, Christie wrote over 60 detective novels and 14 short story collections, selling more than two billion copies worldwide. Her writing is known for clever plots, surprising twists, and carefully placed clues that challenge readers to solve the mystery alongside the detective.


Christie created some of fictions most famous detectives: Hercule Poirot, Miss Jane Marple and Tommy and Tuppence Baresford. Poirot is a brilliant Belgian detective who relies on logic and psychology, while Miss Marple uses her understanding of human nature to uncover the truth. Tommy and Tuppence are unique detectives because the readers age with them unlike the previously mentioned detectives. Through these characters, Christie explored themes of deception, morality, and justice, making her books timeless and popular with readers of all ages.
